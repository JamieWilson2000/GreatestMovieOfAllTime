Just sit back, relax and let me tell you a story....

Once upon a time, long ago in Ancient Greece, there lived a man who, whilst although a slave, was also a fantastic storyteller. He told his stories to the local children on the streets, and to the adults at banquets by means of entertaining.
Through the years the stories have been re-told, re-written and re-loved by people young and old.
Enter into the world of Aesop and learn about the man, log in to enjoy a handful of his most famous fables, have a go at a fun fact quiz, and have a chance to write your own story to add to the many much-loved tales!!
