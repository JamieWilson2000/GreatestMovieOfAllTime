Just sit back, relax and let me tell you a story....

Once upon a time, long ago in Ancient Greece, there lived a man who was a slave, but also a fantastic storyteller. He told his stories to the local children on the streets, and to the adults at banquets by means of entertaining.
Through the years the stories have been re-told, re-written and re-loved by people young and old.
Enter into the world of Aesop and learn about the man, and enjoy a handful of his most famous fables that have been lovingly portrayed throughout centuries and still enjoyed today!
